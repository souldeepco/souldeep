Soul Deep — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Soul Deep is a finger labyrinth meditation app. It is designed to be completely private.

What we collect

Nothing. Soul Deep does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information or usage data. The app contains no analytics, no advertising, and no accounts. Its only network activity is the store's own billing service, and only if you choose to leave an optional tip.

Your journal

Intentions and reflections you write in the app are stored only on your own device, in the app's private storage. They are never uploaded anywhere. Deleting the app deletes them.

Permissions

Soul Deep requests no special permissions. Haptic feedback (vibration) uses standard device capabilities and involves no data.

Optional tips

Soul Deep offers an optional tip jar. Payments are processed entirely by Google Play or the Apple App Store; the app never sees or stores any payment information.

Children

The app is suitable for all ages and collects no data from anyone, including children.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted at this address with a new date.
